Bio

 

A graduate of Humber College’s Creative Photography program, Arlene Repar has been capturing special moments for couples and families since 1992.

With patience, sensitivity and experience, Arlene is able to find what works for each couple in order to bring out the best in your photographs.

It's exciting to see your wedding photos for the first time after the honeymoon, and they grow even more meaningful and treasured over time.

Creativity with a touch of romance, capturing the happiness of your day.
